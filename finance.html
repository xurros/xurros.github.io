Financial Re-engineering Details

Financial Re-engineering is the radical redesign of business processes and organisational structure in order to achieve significant improvements in performance, such as productivity, cost reduction, cycle time, and quality.


Improvements in the finance department can help save money and at the same time provide your organization with more accurate information to make better decisions. Our team of financial reengineering experts, accustomed to working with governments, can evaluate your current financial operations at a fundamental level. They will analyze everything from the skill set of your finance personnel to the adequacy and robustness of your financial reporting systems.


Our professionals will study your organization’s situation and offer custom solutions.


Their goals will be to help you:

  • optimize technology and reduce the amount of manual intervention required in performing financial tasks.

  • Develop a single system for all financial reporting within and outside of the finance department.

  • That means that they will help you eliminate Excel “shadow” spreadsheets, standardize your chart of accounts and related accounting procedures, and automatically generate financial reports.

Comprehensive Financial Solutions

Besides finding opportunities for improvements, our reengineering team can help you implement its recommendations. Because technology often plays an important role in reengineering, you will appreciate our professionals’ nowledge of the software solutions and delivery methods available. They also an evaluate the ability of your infrastructure to support expanded technology.


After the technology decisions have been made, our reengineering experts an help streamline our organization’s financial processes. Often this means eliminating the need for supplemental spreadsheets by creating and tracking a single organizational budget.


They also will look at your policies and procedures. If restructuring is needed, they can create an organizational chart and write appropriate job descriptions that carefully incorporate the segregation of duties to minimize mistakes and protect against fraud.


Accurate Information on a Timely Basis


Often our financial reengineering team can trim an organization’s monthly financial closing process by 50 to 75 percent. They do this by minimizing manual effort and reducing cycle times while at the same time improving data accuracy. Dramatic changes in the economy call for deliberate, carefully developed responses. Your organization will be better able to respond when your finance department is optimized.

Investment Advisory Details

An Investment advisory, in financial/investment organizations, is the unit linking the investment professionals in the central asset management unit (Investment Research, Portfolio Management) to the relationship managers and/or to important clients of the asset management organization.


Investment Advisors explain the investment ideas of the experts to their internal or external clientele and propose adequate investment solutions. In turn, they identify needs and wishes of the clients or the Relationship Managers of their organization and transport them to the central (financial/investment) asset management unit.


As Investment Advisory our business is a critical component of the suite of services provided. We demonstrates our ability to advise large equity funds, with its extensive local knowledge and investment expertise.

Private Equity Arrangement Details

In finance, private equity is an asset class consisting of equity securities in operating companies that are not publicly traded on a stock exchange. A private equity investment will generally be made by a private equity firm, a venture capital firm or an angel investor. Each of these categories of investor has its own set of goals, preferences and investment strategies; each however providing working capital to a target company to nurture expansion, new product development, or restructuring of the company’s operations, management, or ownership.


Private equity is also often grouped into a broader category called private capital, generally used to describe capital supporting any long-term, illiquid investment strategy.


Among the most common investment strategies in private equity are:

  • Leveraged Buyouts
  • Venture Capital
  • Growth Capital
  • Distressed Investments
  • Mezzanine Capital

As Private Equity Arrangement we faciliate all the above arrangments.
