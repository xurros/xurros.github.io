So, Who Are We?


Incorporators UAE is a leader in incorporation and company formation services and a developer of cost- and time-effective business solutions. We deliver the highest quality services to over 350 clients located in more than 20 countries.

The objective of the company is to provide a 'ONE-STOP Solution' for guiding our customers towards managing their businesses better.

Our company provides incorporation/formation of corporations and limited liability companies in UAE and India. We also take care about all incorporated/formed companies by offering organizational, maintenance, administration and accounting services. All services are provided in cooperation with qualified legal and accounting professionals licensed in those jurisdictions.

Incoporators UAE are long term members and registrated business consultants for most of the Free Zones in UAE and manintains the highest A+ ratings.

We meet and exceed the highest industry standards in terms of reliability and quality of the offered services. Our high professionalism, one of the fastest turn-around time in the industry, superior customer service and extremely competitive prices created our trustful and recognizable image in UAE, India and many other countries.

Incorporators UAE, has been structured to provide clients with a complete range of services, which would add value to a Business organization. These services are provided through a dedicated team of professionals with varied industry experience and backgrounds. Incorporators UAE facilitates setting up of businesses in various Government entities in UAE and abroad after understanding the requirements of the clients to create a successful business ventures and environments. We have catered right from a start up Individual to Multi Billion dollar companies like, Euronet World wide - USA, E-Pay -France, ATX Global, AFS Middle East, Envoy Services UK etc. We have branched ourselves in strategic locations through out UAE and have future plans to expand to India and the Middle East.

We strongly believe that knowledgeable clients are able to make right business decisions. Therefore, our website contains detailed and transparent information about offered services and prices. We also offer absolutely free consultations to all our prospects and clients. Anyone could call, email, fax or visit our office during the regular business hours and get valuable information absolutely free.

Unlike of many other offshore service providers, Incorporators UAE is not a reseller of offshore legal entities. Our company has own legal representatives in the most popular offshore jurisdictions. As a result, our clients could get all required services in one place and save valuable time for their businesses. The clients should not worry about missing deadlines for filing of required government returns and payments of government fees. We take care about all those issues and much more.

Testimonials of our clients reflect high level of satisfaction by quality of our services.

Incorporators UAE was incorporated in 2007 and based in Dubai, UAE.
